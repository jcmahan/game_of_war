WAR is a very simple card game. The goal? Get all your opponent's cards. How? Each player turns over one card... highest card value wins! (Deuces are low, Aces high.) If you win battle, you claim the cards on the field. If the cards are tied, a WAR ensues. Each player plays one card face down, and goes to battle again! The highest value wins. First to collect all the cards wins the Total War & achieves world domination.
